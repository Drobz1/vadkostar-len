Policy

1. Use of the Service

  • vadkostarölen.se is a free website that shows beer prices at bars in Stockholm.
  • The information is provided as is, without any guarantee that prices are always up-to-date or accurate.
  • Users are encouraged to double-check with the bar before visiting.

2. Disclaimer

  • We are not responsible for any inaccuracies, price changes, or misunderstandings that may arise from using the website.
  • All prices are collected manually and may change without prior notice.
  • We do not take into account exceptions that apply during holidays, vacation periods, or special events.
  • This site is a hobby project created to help people find cheap beer in Stockholm.

3. Intellectual Property Rights

  • All content on the site - including the logo and design - belongs to vadkostarölen.se.
  • May not be copied, republished, or used for commercial purposes without written permission.

4. Privacy & Location Data

  • We do not collect personal information such as name or email address.
  • We do not use third-party tracking or advertiser cookies.
  • The website may request access to your location to display bars near you.
  • Location data is only used locally in your browser and is not stored by us.
  • You can deny location access - the service will still work but with less precision.
  • We (vadkostarölen.se) use local storage (LocalStorage) in your browser to remember if you have completed our tutorial. This information is stored only locally and is not used for tracking or marketing. You can remove this information at any time by clearing your browser's stored data.

5. About Beer Size

  • We primarily focus on prices for strong beer (40 cl).
  • If other beer types or sizes are stated (e.g. 50 cl, medium beer, or alcohol-free), we try to clarify this in the bar's information.
  • Our priority is to show the cheapest strong beer in standard size for a fair comparison between bars.

6. Corrections & Contact

  • We do our best to keep the information updated.
  • Found an error? Please send us a DM on Instagram: @vadkostarolen.se. We appreciate all help!

7. Policy for Responsible Communication About Alcohol

Vadkostarölen.se is an independent platform that provides price information about beer in bars in Stockholm. Our aim is to provide neutral and factual information to adult users – not to encourage alcohol consumption.

We clearly state the following:

  • Price information only: We only publish data about prices and availability. We do not make product or consumption recommendations.
  • No encouragement to consume: We never encourage increased alcohol intake or drinking in specific situations.
  • Age limit: Our communication is not directed at individuals under 18 years of age.
  • Responsible imagery: We do not display people in connection with alcohol consumption on our platforms.
  • Transparency in collaborations: Any material based on cooperation or compensation from bars or other actors is clearly marked as “collaboration” or “advertisement,” in accordance with applicable law.

Vadkostarölen.se follows Swedish alcohol legislation, marketing law, and the Swedish Consumer Agency's guidelines for advertising and influence in digital media.

8. Changes to the Policy

  • We may update this policy without prior notice.
  • The latest update date is shown at the bottom of the page.
  • We encourage users to read the policy regularly to stay informed.
